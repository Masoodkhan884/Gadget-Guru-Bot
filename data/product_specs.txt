## 🔧 How to Use

### Brewing Coffee:
1. Place a paper or metal filter inside the cap.
2. Attach the filter cap to the chamber.
3. Add 1–2 scoops of ground coffee.
4. Pour hot water (80–95°C) up to marked level.
5. Stir gently for 10 seconds.
6. Insert plunger and press slowly for 20–30 seconds.
7. Serve and enjoy fresh coffee.

---

### How to Clean:
1. Remove filter cap.
2. Push out coffee puck.
3. Rinse all parts with warm water.
4. Wash with mild soap if needed.
5. Dry before next use.

---

### How to Avoid Leakage:
- Ensure filter cap is tightly locked
- Do not overfill water chamber
- Press plunger straight downward

## ⚠️ Troubleshooting

**Problem: Coffee tastes weak**
- Use finer grind size
- Increase coffee quantity
- Extend brewing time slightly

**Problem: Plunger is hard to press**
- Reduce water level
- Check filter blockage
- Stir coffee before pressing

**Problem: Water leaking from bottom**
- Ensure filter cap is properly locked
- Replace rubber seal if worn out
